What Is WaffleWay?

What is WaffleWayFinder?
We help you find hotels that utilize wafflemakers in their breakfast options! We love waffles and want to help you find some on your next hotel stay!

ReservationFAQ

Can I make a reservation using WaffleWay?
While we connect you to waffle-approved hotels, we do not facilitate reservation creation or managment. We do add links to each hotels respective website if you would like to find their availability! Check Out Hotels!

Where can I find recipes for waffles?

Can I find hotels with waffle makers in the rooms?
While we love that concept, this may be a niche offering that we are not capable of filtering out. Perhaps one day we will be able to spread the joy of waffles far and wide for this to be a widely available hotel amenity. Until then you might have more luck on Google

Are gluten-free or vegan waffle options available at these hotels?
Yes, some hotels offer gluten-free or vegan waffle options to cater to different dietary preferences. Make sure to check the hotel details or contact the hotel directly to inquire about specific dietary accommodations.

Can I request waffles for room service at these hotels?
While not all hotels may offer waffles through room service, some do provide this option. Check the hotel's room service menu or contact the front desk to inquire about the availability of waffles for in-room dining.

Can I search for other specialties?
We are passionate about waffles! We don't currently offer any other search filters, but one day we may make a sister site for other loves in the hospitality industry.

Why did you create WaffleWay?
WaffleWay began as a fun conversation that sparked into a reality! Our developers are students who wanted to learn more about Client-Side Web Development.

We're so glad you're here and we hope you enjoy the WaffleWay!

Who created WaffleWay?
The web developers are students from Loyola University Chicago. We created WaffleWay to share our fondness for waffle and to gain a deeper understanding of web development. Like what you see and want to connect with us?
