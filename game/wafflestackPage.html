Hotels Make a Waffle FAQ Game

Instructions

Once the game starts, you'll see a the base of the stack, and the waffle blocks that you need to stack on top of each other will start to fly in. Use the mouse by clicking or the by pressing the spacebar to drop the blocks onto the platform in the position you want. Be careful to stack them as straight and balanced as possible.

Press the button labeled Restart Game to play again!
